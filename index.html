About Rollout AI

Rollout AI is a UK-based AI-driven SaaS platform transforming fibre, utilities, and streetworks infrastructure delivery through predictive decision intelligence.

Powered by the proprietary Rollout Intelligence Engine (RIE), the platform integrates Street Manager permit feeds, PIA datasets, subcontractor productivity benchmarks, and council-specific constraints into one unified optimisation engine.

By replacing static planning with probability-weighted optimisation, Rollout AI enables operators to reduce delays, prevent repeat excavations, improve regulatory compliance, and accelerate national infrastructure rollouts by 20–30%.
